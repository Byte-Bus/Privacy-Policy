# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 6, 2026

**Effective Date:** April 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Byte Bus ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and shares your information when you use the TOS mobile application ("App"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

- **Phone number** (used as your primary login credential)
- **Username** (your chosen display name)
- **Password** (stored in hashed form; we never store or have access to your plaintext password)

### 1.2 Profile Information

You may choose to provide additional profile information, including:

- Display name
- Bio / about me text
- Gender
- Date of birth
- Country
- Languages spoken
- Profile photo (avatar)
- Cover photos (up to 6)
- Voice introduction sample

If you register as a Buddy (service provider), you may also provide:

- Gaming experience and rank information
- Listings describing the services you offer, including game titles, pricing, and descriptions
- Availability schedule

### 1.3 Device Information

When you use the App, we automatically collect:

- **Device identifier** (a unique ID generated for your device)
- **Device type** (e.g., Android, iOS)
- **Device name** (if provided by your device or manually entered)
- **Push notification token** (for delivering notifications to your device)

### 1.4 Financial and Transaction Information

When you use our coin-based wallet system, we collect:

- Wallet balance and transaction history (top-ups, purchases, earnings, refunds)
- Top-up order details, including the amount and payment proof you upload (e.g., receipt screenshots)
- Session order details (bookings you create or fulfill)
- Gift transaction records (coins sent to or received from other users)
- Payout records (for Buddies earning coins from completed sessions)

We do not collect or store credit card numbers, bank account numbers, or other direct payment instrument details within the App.

### 1.5 Chat and Communication Data

When you use in-app messaging, we collect:

- Text messages, images, voice messages, videos, stickers, and gift notifications you send or receive
- Chat room metadata (participants, creation time, last activity)
- Read receipts (when you read a message)
- Messages exchanged with our customer support team

### 1.6 Social and Community Data

When you interact with social features, we collect:

- Moments (posts) you create, including text and images
- Comments and likes on moments
- Your follower and following lists
- Reviews and ratings you leave for other users
- Reports you submit about other users, listings, moments, or messages

### 1.7 Usage Data

We collect operational data to maintain and improve the App:

- Online/offline status and last-seen timestamps
- Session and booking activity (times, durations, completion status)
- App interaction logs for troubleshooting and service reliability

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- **Provide the Service** -- create and manage your account, process bookings between Clients and Buddies, facilitate in-app communication, and operate the coin wallet system
- **Deliver Notifications** -- send push notifications about new messages, booking updates, order status changes, and system announcements
- **Ensure Safety and Security** -- verify accounts, detect fraud, enforce our Terms of Service, and respond to user reports
- **Moderate Content** -- review reported content (profiles, listings, moments, messages) to maintain community standards
- **Process Transactions** -- manage coin top-ups, session payments, gift transfers, buddy payouts, and maintain financial records
- **Improve the Service** -- analyze usage patterns, diagnose technical issues, and develop new features
- **Provide Support** -- respond to your inquiries through our in-app support chat

---

## 3. How We Store Your Information

### 3.1 Remote Storage

Your data is stored on our servers using secure databases, with each service area (authentication, profiles, bookings, chat, payments, reviews) maintaining its own dedicated storage. Media files (photos, voice samples, videos) are stored using cloud storage and delivered through a content delivery network (CDN).

### 3.2 Local Storage on Your Device

The App stores certain data locally on your device:

- **Authentication tokens** are stored in your device's secure storage (Android Keystore) to keep you logged in
- **Chat messages** are cached locally for offline access and faster loading
- **App preferences** (such as theme settings) are stored in local shared preferences
- **Temporary media files** may be stored briefly in your device's cache before upload

### 3.3 Data Retention

- **Account data** is retained for as long as your account exists
- **Chat messages** are stored on our servers while your account is active
- **Transaction records** are retained indefinitely for financial auditing and dispute resolution
- **Device tokens** are retained until you log out
- **Uploaded media** is retained until you delete it or your account is closed
- **Local cache** is cleared when you log out of the App

## 4. How We Share Your Information

### 4.1 With Other Users

Certain information is visible to other users as part of the App's functionality:

- Your profile information (display name, avatar, bio, country, languages, gender, voice sample, cover photos) is visible to other users who view your profile
- If you are a Buddy, your listings, ratings, average review scores, session count, and moments are publicly visible within the App
- Chat messages are visible to the other participant(s) in the conversation
- Reviews and ratings you leave are visible to the reviewed user and other users
- Your online status is visible to other users

### 4.2 With Service Providers

We use third-party services to operate the App, including:

- **Cloud messaging providers** for delivering push notifications
- **Cloud storage providers** for hosting uploaded media files

These providers only receive data necessary to perform their specific function and are bound by their own privacy policies.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

### 4.3 For Legal Reasons

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or if we believe in good faith that such action is necessary to:

- Comply with a legal obligation, court order, or governmental request
- Protect and defend the rights or property of Byte Bus
- Prevent fraud or address security issues
- Protect the personal safety of users or the public

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

### 5.1 Access and Update

You can view and update your profile information, including your display name, bio, avatar, cover photos, voice sample, gender, date of birth, country, and languages at any time through the App.

### 5.2 Delete Your Account

You may request deletion of your account by contacting us. Upon account deletion, we will:

- Remove your profile, listings, and moments from public visibility
- Delete your authentication credentials and device tokens
- Retain transaction records as required by law for financial auditing purposes

### 5.3 Notifications

You can manage push notification permissions through your device's system settings. Disabling notifications will not affect core App functionality, but you may miss important updates about bookings and messages.

### 5.4 Media

You can delete your profile photos, cover photos, and voice sample at any time through the App's profile editing features.

## 8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including:

- Encrypting authentication tokens using your device's secure storage (Android Keystore)
- Hashing passwords before storage (we never store plaintext passwords)
- Transmitting all data over encrypted HTTPS connections
- Implementing rate limiting to prevent abuse
- Using role-based access controls to restrict data access

No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
